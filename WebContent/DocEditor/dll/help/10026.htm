View Final

The Document Comparison Final screen initially presents the template as it is in your Target version, with all changes between it and the selected Source as yet to be either Accepted or Rejected. As you Accept or Reject changes, the Final view is updated to reflect these decisions.

The Differences panel to the left of the Final template panel presents a summary of the differences detected between the Source and Final.

The Differences entry for the change that is currently in focus is expanded, displayed in color, and has bold borders; its expanded label indicates the nature of the change. The change is further highlighted in the Final view, with the affected fragment highlighted between bold horizontal lines.

In addition, a 'quick navigation' bar parallel with the vertical scroll bar, indicates the relative positions of the various changes in the document with pale blue blocks of color. The block for the current change is outlined in bold or, where the block is very narrow, solid dark blue. To jump to a particular change, simply click on its color block.
